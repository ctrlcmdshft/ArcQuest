## Tips for Quest Completion

1. **Multi-Location Quests**: Some quests require visiting multiple maps in a single round. Plan your route carefully to maximize efficiency.

2. **Key Items**: Many quests reward you with keys (Raider Hatch Keys, Building Keys) that unlock additional loot locations. Prioritize these quests early.

3. **Blueprints**: Quests that reward blueprints (like Trigger Nade, Lure Grenade, Hullcracker) are valuable as they allow you to craft these items later.

4. **ARC Enemy Quests**: Quests requiring specific ARC enemy kills can be completed opportunistically during regular exploration.

5. **Supply and Crafting Materials**: Many quests reward valuable crafting materials like Advanced Mechanical Components, which are essential for upgrades.

## Quest Categories

### Combat Quests
Focus on destroying ARC enemies and collecting their components:
- Clearer Skies
- The Right Tool
- The Trifecta
- Out of the Shadows
- Into the Fray

### Exploration Quests
Require finding and visiting specific locations:
- Picking Up The Pieces
- Broken Monument
- Market Correction
- With a Trace

### Collection Quests
Task you with gathering specific items or materials:
- Doctor's Orders
- Trash Into Treasure
- Building a Library
- A Bad Feeling

### Installation/Repair Quests
Involve repairing or activating equipment:
- Off The Radar
- Hatch Repairs
- After Rain Comes
- Flickering Threat

### Delivery Quests
Require delivering items to NPCs:
- Industrial Espionage
- Tribute to Toledo
- The Major's Footlocker
- Espresso
